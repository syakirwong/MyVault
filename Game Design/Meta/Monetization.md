# Monetization

---

## Core Principle

NO PAY-TO-WIN. Period. Power is earned through gameplay only.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Cosmetic Shop

- Title suffixes, chat flair, build card borders, profile banners, combat log styles, emotes
- See [[Cosmetic System]] for full catalog and pricing
- Estimated ARPPU: $8-12/month for paying players
- Target conversion rate: 5-8% of active players

### 2. Battle Pass (Seasonal)

- $10 per 3-month season
- Cosmetic-only rewards track (30 tiers)
- Free track available (10 tiers)
- No gameplay advantage
- Includes: Seasonal title suffix, chat flair set, emotes, build card border, profile banner

### 3. Character Slots

- 2 free character slots
- Additional slots: $5 each
- Max 6 slots

### 4. Premium UI Customization

- Premium text effects for combat log styling
- Custom build card themes
- Cannot affect gameplay information display

---

## What We Will NEVER Sell

- Gems or gem power
- Experience boosters
- Gold or currency
- Gear with stats
- Inventory expansion that affects gameplay
- Anything that gives combat advantage

---

## Monetization Health Metrics

| Metric | Healthy | Warning |
|--------|---------|---------|
| % revenue from whales (top 1%) | < 40% | > 60% |
| Avg spend per paying user | $8-15/month | > $30/month |
| Non-paying player retention vs paying | Within 10% | > 20% gap |
| Community pay-to-win sentiment | < 5% complaints | > 15% |

# Gem Identity System

There are no classes. Your gems ARE your class.

Each player equips 4 gems from a pool of domains. The combination of domains defines your systemic rules of engagement, your action draw pool, your resonance possibilities, and your emergent archetype name. Two players with the same 4 gems still play differently based on mutation paths, resonance discoveries, and tactical choices.

---

## Gem Domains

Each domain defines three things:

1. **Systemic Rule** — how ALL your actions behave when this gem is equipped
2. **Action Pool** — 3-4 base actions this gem contributes to your draw
3. **Mutation Tendency** — how this gem changes under stress

### 8 MVP Domains

---

### Kinetics

> *"Force ripples outward. The space between you collapses."*

**Systemic Rule:** All your actions have displacement effects. Hits push, misses pull. Positioning determines damage bonuses. The battlefield is never static.

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Force Strike | Damage + push target 1 position away |
| Gravity Well | Pull target 1 position closer, slow |
| Momentum Burst | Damage scales with total displacement this round |
| Deflection Field | Redirect next incoming damage to adjacent position |

**Mutation Tendency:** Displacement distances shift. Push becomes pull, pull becomes push. Gravity inverts.

---

### Entropy

> *"The edges of reality fray. Something fundamental gives way."*

**Systemic Rule:** Your actions erode. Everything you touch degrades over time. Debuffs applied by you last 50% longer and stack one tier deeper than normal.

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Corrode | Damage + reduce target's defense for 2 rounds |
| Decay Chain | DoT that spreads to second target if unresolved |
| Destabilize | Increase target gem's instability by 15 |
| Unravel | Strip one active buff from target |

**Mutation Tendency:** Self-erosion begins. Your own buffs decay faster. Your debuffs become more potent but start affecting you.

---

### Resonance

> *"The first note of something larger. The echo answers."*

**Systemic Rule:** Your actions echo. Every action produces a diminished copy that repeats 1 round later at 40% power. Repeating the same action type amplifies echoes instead of diminishing them.

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Harmonic Strike | Damage + place echo marker on target |
| Amplify | Double the power of your next echo |
| Frequency Shift | Move all pending echoes forward (trigger immediately) or backward (delay 1 round) |
| Dampening Wave | Cancel all echoes and buffs on target (yours and theirs) |

**Mutation Tendency:** Echoes become unpredictable. May fire at wrong times, at wrong targets, or at amplified/reduced power.

---

### Void

> *"The void remembers nothing. Where the strike lands, nothing remains."*

**Systemic Rule:** Your actions negate. You can consume opponent's buffs to fuel your own damage, create dead zones where actions fail, and temporarily remove yourself from existence.

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Consume | Damage + absorb one buff from target (converts to your resource) |
| Null Zone | Create a zone for 2 rounds where all actions fail |
| Siphon | Steal 20% of target's current resource pool |
| Absence | Become untargetable for 1 phase, but cannot act |

**Mutation Tendency:** Void hunger grows. Starts consuming your own buffs and constructs. Siphon begins draining your own resources.

---

### Flux

> *"It was fire, then it was ice, then it was something without a name."*

**Systemic Rule:** Your actions transform. Damage type, target count, and secondary effects shift based on context — specifically, the opponent's last action determines what your next action becomes. You are inherently unpredictable.

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Shift Strike | Damage; type and bonus effect mirrors opponent's last action |
| Metamorphosis | Replace one gem's current action set with a random alternative from that domain |
| Adaptive Shield | Block; resistance type matches whatever damage is incoming |
| Phase Walk | Reposition unpredictably; opponent cannot target you with their next positional action |

**Mutation Tendency:** Extreme identity drift. Gem domain rules start bleeding into other equipped gems. The boundary between your gems dissolves.

---

### Tempest

> *"The storm builds. One, two, three — the rhythm becomes a weapon."*

**Systemic Rule:** Your actions chain. Landing hits builds Chain stacks (max 5). Each stack increases your speed priority and adds bonus damage. Missing an action resets ALL stacks to zero.

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Storm Strike | Damage + gain 1 Chain stack |
| Lightning Chain | Damage; bonus damage equal to 15% per Chain stack |
| Overclock | Your next action resolves first regardless of speed |
| Surge | Spend all Chain stacks; deal burst damage equal to 30% per stack consumed |

**Mutation Tendency:** Chain maintenance threshold increases. Requires consecutive hits to maintain stacks. Chain stacks begin decaying naturally.

---

### Aegis

> *"The shield holds. What didn't kill you is about to kill them."*

**Systemic Rule:** Your actions reflect. Damage blocked or absorbed generates Counter-Energy (CE). CE is your offensive resource — the more you endure, the harder you hit back. Defense IS offense.

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Fortify | Reduce next incoming damage by 50%, store blocked amount as CE |
| Reflect | Deal damage equal to stored CE, reset CE to 0 |
| Endure | Absorb ALL damage this phase, add 100% to CE (but you take full damage to Integrity) |
| Sanctuary | Heal Integrity equal to 30% of stored CE |

**Mutation Tendency:** CE storage cap shifts. Reflection efficiency changes. Endure may stop converting damage to CE cleanly.

---

### Synthesis

> *"Something new exists where there was nothing. The pieces remember their shape."*

**Systemic Rule:** Your actions create persistent constructs — zones, wards, or autonomous effects that operate independently for 2-3 rounds. Your power scales with active construct count. More constructs = more options.

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Construct | Create a persistent zone (damage, buff, or debuff) lasting 3 rounds |
| Enhance | Increase one active construct's potency by 50% |
| Merge | Combine two active constructs into one stronger construct with both effects |
| Catalyze | Trigger all active constructs simultaneously for burst effect, then they expire |

**Mutation Tendency:** Constructs become unstable. May merge unexpectedly, collapse early, or produce unintended effects.

## Gem Loadout

- **4 gem slots** — each filled with a different domain
- No duplicate domains
- Each gem contributes 3-4 actions to your **draw pool** (~12-16 total actions)
- With 8 domains and 4 slots: **70 possible loadouts**
- Each loadout produces **6 resonance pairs** to discover

### Optional: Overload Slot (Post-MVP)

High-level content may allow a temporary 5th gem slot. This dramatically increases combinatorial options but introduces instability across all equipped gems. The 5th gem starts at 50 stability instead of 100.

## Resonance System

When two gems are equipped together, their systemic rules can interact to produce **resonance effects** — emergent bonuses that neither gem describes on its own.

### Resonance Discovery

- Resonances are **not documented in-game**
- Players discover them through experimentation, dungeon exploration, and PvP
- Community knowledge builds shared lore around "known" resonances
- Some resonances require specific action sequences to trigger
- Some require environmental conditions (dungeon gem ecology)

### Resonance Tiers

| Tier | Trigger | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Passive** | Just having both gems equipped | Kinetics + Tempest: Chain stacks also increase displacement distance |
| **Active** | Using actions from both gems in sequence | Entropy + Void: Corrode → Consume creates "Annihilate" — destroy one buff AND apply defense debuff |
| **Conditional** | Requires specific game state | Resonance + Synthesis: 3+ echoes active + 2+ constructs = "Harmonic Engine" — all constructs gain echo |
| **Legendary** | Extremely specific conditions, rare | Flux + Entropy + environmental mutation trigger: "Unraveling" — all gems on the field (yours and opponent's) lose 10 stability |

### Resonance Volume

- **MVP:** ~80 resonances across all domain pairs (28 pairs × ~3 avg)
- **Post-MVP:** Expand to 200+ with seasonal/patch additions
- **Procedural layer:** Seasonal modifiers shift which conditional resonances are available, keeping the meta fresh

## Emergent Archetype Naming

The system dynamically generates your archetype name based on three layers:

### Layer 1: Domain Combination (Base Archetype)

Your 4 equipped domains produce a compound name. The system prioritizes the two domains most used in recent combat.

| Example Loadout | Base Archetype |
|----------------|---------------|
| Kinetics + Tempest + Entropy + Void | **Stormkinetic** |
| Aegis + Resonance + Synthesis + Void | **Resonant Architect** |
| Flux + Entropy + Tempest + Kinetics | **Chaosweaver** |
| Aegis + Kinetics + Synthesis + Resonance | **Harmonic Bastion** |

### Layer 2: Playstyle Modifier (Sub-Archetype)

Based on actual combat behavior over recent sessions:

| Playstyle | Modifier | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Aggressive | "Assault" / "Breaker" | >60% offensive actions committed |
| Defensive | "Guardian" / "Warden" | >60% defensive/reactive actions committed |
| Adaptive | "Shifter" / "Flux" | High action variety, frequent gem switches |
| Controller | "Architect" / "Weaver" | Heavy construct/zone/debuff usage |
| Burst | "Storm" / "Surge" | Frequent chain spending and high-commit plays |

### Layer 3: Resonance Title (Prestige)

Discovering rare resonances permanently unlocks prestige modifiers:

- First Legendary resonance discovered: unique title (e.g., "of the Unraveling")
- 10+ Active resonances mastered: domain mastery title
- Full-set resonance (all 6 pairs active in one fight): "Resonance Master" prefix

### Full Name Example

```
[Corrupted] ★★★ Chaosweaver Assault Kael of the Unraveling
```

## Stats

With no classes, stats emerge from gem domains and character level.

| Stat | Effect | Primary Domains |
|------|--------|----------------|
| Force | Damage scaling, displacement power | Kinetics, Tempest |
| Precision | Crit chance, action accuracy, speed priority | Tempest, Flux |
| Resilience | Integrity pool, damage reduction | Aegis, Synthesis |
| Acuity | Debuff potency, resource efficiency, log detail | Entropy, Void |
| Attunement | Echo power, construct duration, resonance trigger chance | Resonance, Synthesis |
| Instinct | Draw pool size bonus, conditional action availability | Flux, Kinetics |

Each equipped gem provides base stats weighted toward its primary stats. Character level provides a flat bonus across all stats.
